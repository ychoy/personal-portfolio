Yan-Yin Choy

Junior Full Stack Developer

I am a Junior Full Stack Developer looking for an exciting opportunity to build products that transform the way we collaborate, connect and live.

I have been collaborating with Code for San José to build an affordable housing web application, using Rails, Ember.js and PostgreSQL. I enjoy working across the stack and building web applications. I recently graduated from General Assembly - Web Development Immersive, where I learned to build full stack web applications with JavaScript, MongoDB, Node.js, Express, and Ruby on Rails. I utilized best practices including agile development, pair programming and object-oriented programming principles.

I am engaged in other projects to grow my skills in full stack development. In order to grow my knowledge of WebSockets, I am using Action Cable to improve the direct messaging feature for WeHuddle. To practice making an enriching web experience, I am using React.js to build a prototype for a renters rights app. Lastly, I am practicing schema migrations by changing and expanding datasets with Rails Active Record and PostgreSQL.

In my prior career in nonprofit management, I managed civic tech and civic impact projects. I engaged government clients to develop project scope. I also crafted project roadmaps, recruited tech talent, and coordinated project meetings. I bring eight years of experience in problem-solving, communications and project management.
