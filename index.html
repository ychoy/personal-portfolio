Yan-Yin Choy

Junior Full Stack Developer

I am a creative and versatile Junior Full Stack Developer looking for an exciting opportunity to build products that transform the way we collaborate, connect and live. I have a knack for design and enjoy working across the stack.

I collaborate with Code for San José to build an affordable housing web application with Rails, Ember.js and PostgreSQL. I enjoy working across the stack. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive bootcamp, where I learned to build full stack web applications with JavaScript, MongoDB, Node.js, Express, and Ruby on Rails individually and in teams. I utilized best practices including pair programming and agile development.

I am engaged in other projects to grow my skills in full stack development. To practice making an enriching web experience, I am using React.js to build a prototype for a renters rights app. Lastly, I am practicing schema migrations by changing and expanding datasets with Rails Active Record and PostgreSQL.

In my prior career in nonprofit management, I engaged government clients and tech talent to innovate government services. I developed project scope, crafted project roadmaps, recruited tech talent, and coordinated meetings. I bring eight years of experience in problem-solving, communications and project management.

If you would like to collaborate, connect with me.
