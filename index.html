Hi! I am a multi-disciplined software engineer with a focus on building products that solve problems. I work strategically with cross-functional teams to create web applications. I enjoy building simple solutions. I am driven to learn quickly and make sustainable impact. This is what I love to do, and I am interested in doing this for you.

I collaborated with engineers in Brazil to build an open source web portal for librarians to manage data with React, GraphQL and Apollo Client. I lead planning meetings and help the team strategize.

Over the last decade, I leveraged my effective communications, management and analytical skills to lead and deliver with entrepreneurial teams. I have been volunteering with Code for San José for the past four years. As Brigade Captain, I coordinate bi-weekly Civic Hack meetings. I also led a team to build Renter’s Rights Guide web application with Rails and React. Previously as a nonprofit manager, I facilitated product development and programs to innovate government services. Additionally, I executed fundraising strategy and supervised interns.
