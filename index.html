Yan-Yin Choy

Full Stack Developer

Hi! As a Full Stack Developer, I want to transform the way we collaborate, connect, and live through the products we use on a daily basis. As Product Development Intern at Caravan Studios, I build apps that empower communities to connect, access and organize resources. I collaborate with Code for San José on the Renter's Rights Guide app, which is built with Rails, React and PostgreSQL.

I graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive bootcamp, where I learned to build web applications with JavaScript, MongoDB, Node.js, Express, Angular, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL. In my prior career in nonprofit management, I translated client requirements into technical specifications, defined project scope, and recruited and managed tech talent on civic impact projects. I earned a Bachelor's Degree in Environmental Studies from San José State University.

I'd love to learn more about your goals and challenges as I seek opportunities to leverage eight years of problem-solving experience, clear communication, and project management. Let's chat about collaboration! You can reach me by email.
